The Rules

The rules are simple, a random order of cards. Click a card and it will flip and show a picture of a famous building or place. When you click a second card it too will flip and display its image. If the cards match they will stay face up. If they don't the cards match the cards will flip back over when you click another card or when the cards have stayed up for a time based on difficulty level.

Difficulty

Difficulty is chosen by the player and sets the timer and the time cards stay face up.

  • Easy: Timer 60 seconds, cards flip back after 1 second
  • Medium: Timer 45 seconds, cards flip back after 0.5 seconds
  • Hard: Timer 30 seconds, cards flip back after 0.25 seconds

Levels

Level 1 starts at four pairs, two more pairs will be added for each new level. Five seconds will be added to the timer for each level.

Score

10 points are added to score for each pair, 2 points are subtratced from score after each incorrect pair. Total score is calculated by multiplying the score with a time multiplier and a difficulty multiplier. The previous levels score carries over to the next level and is added to the score of the current level.
